                      <h1>A better way to manage your learning applications and educational technology</h1>
					  <p>Research shows that a more engaged learner is more successful, in the classroom and beyond. That’s why <a href="http://www.pearsonmylabandmastering.com/northamerica/index.html" rel="ext">MyLab &amp; Mastering</a>, Pearson’s leading educational technology, is designed to engage and thus help learners reach specific, measurable learning outcomes. MyLabsPlus is the premier enterprise solution that offers a simple way to deliver the engaging experiences that lead to better results across your entire institution.</p>
 
					  <p>MyLabsPlus can be connected to your school’s student information system and learning management system, enabling educators and administrators to centrally manage, enroll, and report on student progress across multiple MyLab &amp; Mastering courses. Rich and flexible reporting allows for monitoring of student progress across your courses, department, and institution. And single sign-on from your customized campus portal or your hosted LMS enables easy, convenient access to MyLab &amp; Mastering learning resources on a campuswide scale.</p>
