        <div id="introductionContent" class="content cf">
            <div class="contentRow">
            	<p><strong>Airlines need to generate as much revenue as possible from every flight. That's where we come in.</strong></p>
                
                <p>The Plusgrade revenue upgrade platform enables our partners to offer travelers the opportunity to upgrade their travel experience, if upgraded inventory is available before time of departure.</p> 

				<p>By monetizing premium inventory and services that would otherwise go unused, our partners i) see a high-margin incremental revenue stream delivered to their businesses and ii) strengthen their passenger relationships through increased customer value.</p>

<p>The unique core architecture and algorithms that make up Plusgrade's foundation ensures that each passenger targeted for the opportunity and selected for an upgrade meets a host of internally prioritized goals.</p>

                
            </div>
            
            <div class="contentRow">
                <h3>Program:</h3>
                <ul class="contentlist">
                    <li>Delivered post-reservation confirmation through multiple touch points</li>
                    <li>The upgrade is not guaranteed and subject to availability</li>
                    <li>Passengers only pay if upgraded, otherwise they keep their original ticket</li>
                    <li>Inventory available for sale at full rate is not impacted</li>
                    <li>Passengers never leave the airline’s environment (custom white-label solution)</li>
                </ul>
                
                <h3>Result:</h3>
                <ul class="contentlist">
                	<li>Pure profit direct to your bottom line</li>
                	<li>Engaging and valuable customer experience</li>
            	</ul>
                
                <h3>More:</h3>
                <ul class="contentlist">
                	<li>Easy to implement</li>
                    <li>Interfaces with most reservation systems</li>
                    <li>Support for existing FFP/loyalty programs
                </ul>	
                    
            </div>

        <div id="howItWorksContent" class="content cf">
            
            <ol class="contentRow tworows">
            
            <li>After travelers book their flights, they are offered the opportunity, post-confirmation, to request an upgrade, which is not confirmed until and if airline chooses to award it</li>
            
            <li>Travelers can be engaged via multiple channels including the booking engine confirmation page, the booking confirmation email, a standalone email, or at the airline's website</li>
            
            <li>
            Travelers are directed to an airline-branded web page where they can make an offer to upgrade in an easy 3-step process:            	
             	<ul class="contentlist">
            		<li>Request</li>
					<li>Payment Information</li>
					<li>Review & Submit</li>
            	</ul>
            
            </li>

            
            <li>
            	If the upgrade is awarded, travelers are notified (and quite happy!). Otherwise, travelers retain their original reservation.
            </li>
            
          </ol>

       <div id="benefitsContent" class="content cf">
            <div class="contentRow tworows">
            	
            	<p><strong>Intelligent Targeting Engine</strong><br>
            	Airlines have complete control and flexibility in defining the exact strategy and circumstances under which travelers are offered the upgrade opportunity.
            	</p>

				<p><strong>Powerful Decision Engine</strong><br>
            	Our unique ranking algorithms ensure that only passengers who meet a host of pre-defined goals are awarded the upgrade. Airlines can integrate their Revenue Management, Loyalty, or other important organizational objectives into the ultimate decision.
            	</p>
            	
            	<p><strong>No Risk</strong><br>
            	We share only in the revenue that Plusgrade generates for you.
            	</p>
            	
            	<p><strong>Turnkey Implementation</strong><br>
            	Software as a service that easily interfaces with most reservations systems
            	</p>
            	
            	<p><strong>White-Label Branding</strong><br>
            	Each implementation is customized, fully branded, and white-labeled for our unique airline customers. It's <i>your</i> upgrade program and will look and feel that way.
            	</p>
