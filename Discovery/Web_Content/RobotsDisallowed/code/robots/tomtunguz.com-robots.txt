<h1 id="title"><a style="text-decoration:none" href="/payback_period_cash/">The Importance of Payback Period for SaaS Startups</a></h1>
        <img src="http://tomtunguz.com/images/monthly_working_capital_requirements.png" width=100%/><p>One of the most powerful levers for SaaS companies to master is payback period. Payback period is the number of months a company requires to payback its cost of customer acquisition. The median SaaS startup has a payback period of 15 months. A short payback period confers two massive advantage to a startups - smaller working capital requirements and a consequent ability to grow much faster. </p>
