			<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://blog.dota2.com/2015/09/reborn-has-arrived/" title="Permalink to Reborn has arrived" rel="bookmark">Reborn has arrived</a></h2>

			<div class="entry-meta">
				September 11, 2015 - Dota Team			</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

				<div class="entry-content">
				<p>As of yesterday, all Dota 2 players are now running the Reborn update, which includes the Source 2 engine, a rebuilt UI, and Custom Games. Over the coming weeks we&#8217;ll be updating the game frequently to fix issues and refine the experience based on your feedback. You&#8217;ll be able to find patch notes in the <a href="http://store.steampowered.com/news/18361">usual place</a> once again. </p>
<p><img src="http://cdn.dota2.com/apps/dota2/posts/rebornupdate_blogimage.jpg" alt="" /></p>
<p>In today&#8217;s update we&#8217;ve included a design-related change to the Dashboard. We&#8217;ve integrated the friends list in the Home screen, while still having it be available globally. We debated making this change during the earlier part of the beta, but we didn’t have enough confidence that it was correct until we got more feedback following yesterday&#8217;s release.</p>
<p><a href="http://store.steampowered.com/news/18361">Click here</a> to read up on all the other fixes and improvements that are part of this update. </p>

			<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://blog.dota2.com/2015/09/introducing-dota-levels/" title="Permalink to Introducing Dota Levels" rel="bookmark">Introducing Dota Levels</a></h2>

			<div class="entry-meta">
				September 8, 2015 - Dota Team			</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

				<div class="entry-content">
				<p>As part of today&#8217;s update we&#8217;re unveiling Dota Levels, the culmination of all of your accomplishments in Dota. Dota Levels are displayed as a badge on your Dota Profile based on your level, and are gained by earning trophies and playing matches. You can learn more about Dota Levels <a href="http://www.dota2.com/levels/">here</a>.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.dota2.com/levels/"><img src="http://cdn.dota2.com/apps/dota2/images/blogfiles/blog_dota_levels.jpg" alt="" width="100%" /></a></p>
<p>We&#8217;re also releasing the new Treasury and enabling the Tournaments area of the Watch section. The Treasury can be found in the Heroes section, and allows you to quickly browse, explore, and purchase treasures. The Tournaments area is where you&#8217;ll be able to view matches and tournament details. We&#8217;ve also made a number of other small adjustments and bug fixes, which you can read about <a href="http://www.dota2.com/reborn/updates/">here</a>.</p>
<p>Today we are rolling out a preload of the Reborn Update to all players that have not previously downloaded it. The Reborn Update will be coming to the main client for all Dota 2 players in the next day or two.</p>
<p>Additionally, if you purchased a Compendium for The International 2015, today you will receive the exclusive Harvest, Pestilence, and Sirocco Weather Effect items. We are still working on the Axe Comic/Immortal, as well as doing some tech work needed to support the Desert Terrain item. They will be granted to all Compendium owners as soon as they are ready.</p>

			<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://blog.dota2.com/2015/09/coming-soon-the-reborn-update/" title="Permalink to Coming Soon: The Reborn Update" rel="bookmark">Coming Soon: The Reborn Update</a></h2>

			<div class="entry-meta">
				September 1, 2015 - Dota Team			</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

				<div class="entry-content">
				<p><a href="http://www.dota2.com/reborn/updates/"><img src="http://cdn.dota2.com/apps/dota2/images/blogfiles/reborn_coming_soon.jpg" alt="" width="100%" /></a></p>
<p>With The International behind us and the Majors just around the corner, we are excited to have the world start playing Dota in the brand-new Source 2 engine. Within the coming weeks, the main client will receive the Reborn Update. Moving away from the Source 1 engine soon will allow us to better focus our efforts entirely on Source 2 in preparation for various upcoming tournaments. There are still a few features that will need some work when the update is released, but we plan to finish them quickly. Expect the weeks that follow to focus on ironing out issues, refining the dashboard and iterating based on your feedback. Afterwards, we&#8217;ll begin shifting our attention toward the in-game UI, where we plan to redesign the HUD and work on a variety of other in-game features. </p>
<p><a href="http://www.dota2.com/reborn/updates/">Today&#8217;s update to Reborn</a> contains various bug fixes and improvements, as well as a large number of backend changes that result in a very large one-time download. Now would be a good time for everyone to start downloading the Reborn update, as you will not need to download it again when the main client receives the update in the coming weeks. </p>
<p>If you do not have the Reborn update installed and would like to get a head start on it now, launch the Dota client and head over to the Play Tab to get set up. All of your items are now in the Reborn client, and after today any new content will be added directly to Source 2 only.</p>

			<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://blog.dota2.com/2015/08/prepare-for-the-majors/" title="Permalink to Prepare for The Majors" rel="bookmark">Prepare for The Majors</a></h2>

			<div class="entry-meta">
				August 21, 2015 - Dota Team			</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

				<div class="entry-content">
				<p><a href="http://www.dota2.com/majorsregistration/"><img src="http://cdn.dota2.com/apps/dota2/images/blogfiles/blog_team_registration.jpg" alt="" width="100%" /></a></p>
<p>This fall, a new tradition comes to the world of professional Dota. The Majors are a series of four seasonal marquee tournaments culminating in The International. The first Major will be occuring in Europe this November and we&#8217;ll have more details on ticketing soon.</p>
<p>Ahead of this Major, there will be Open Qualifiers from October 6-9, and Regional Qualifiers from October 10-13. Any team may participate in an Open Qualifier, and two teams from each region will be among those invited to participate in the Regional Qualifiers. The Regional Qualifiers will then determine several of the teams to be invited to battle at the Major. Direct invitations to the Major and the Regional Qualifiers will be announced on October 5th.</p>
<p>If your team would like to be considered for an invitation to either the Regional Qualifiers or the Major directly, head over to the <a href="http://www.dota2.com/majorsregistration/">registration site</a> for more information. Any team that makes changes to its roster after September 5th 2015 at 12:00AM PDT cannot be directly invited to the Regional Qualifiers or Major, and must compete in the Open Qualifiers.</p>

			<div class="entry-meta">
				August 11, 2015 - Dota Team			</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

				<div class="entry-content">
				<p><a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/dota2ti/"><img src="http://cdn.dota2.com/apps/dota2/images/blogfiles/blog_ti5_champs.jpg" alt="" width="100%" /></a></p>
<p>Congratulations to Evil Geniuses, Grand Champions at The International 2015.</p>
<p>It&#8217;s been a long road for the victors. Battling in the Group Stage, Evil Geniuses secured a top position within their grouping. However, at the same time, the underdogs of CDEC Gaming were making a name for themselves. A runner-up at the China Qualifier, CDEC fought their way into the tournament through the Wild Card, and were ultimately tied with Evil Geniuses by the end the Group Stage.</p>
<p>At the Main Event, CDEC dominated the upper bracket, winning every match, eventually knocking Evil Geniuses to the lower bracket to advance to the Grand Finals. In the final day of The International, Evil Geniuses beat LGD Gaming to earn their way to the Grand Finals through the lower bracket, securing a rematch against CDEC Gaming in the process.</p>
<p>In the Grand Finals, CDEC&#8217;s win streak finally came to an end. As the dust settled, it was Evil Geniuses who stood triumphant, defeating CDEC Gaming 3-1.</p>
<p>A new Grand Champion has been crowned, and a new inscription will be set upon the Aegis of Champions:</p>
<p align="center"><strong>2015<br />
Evil Geniuses<br />
Clinton &#8220;Fear&#8221; Loomis<br />
Syed &#8220;Suma1l&#8221; Hassan<br />
Saahil &#8220;UNiVeRsE&#8221; Arora<br />
Kurtis &#8220;Aui_2000&#8243; Ling<br />
Peter &#8220;ppd&#8221; Dager</strong></p>
<p>There were more great stories and incredible games at The International than we can possibly recount. If you&#8217;d like to relive the action, head to <a href="http://www.dota2.com/international/overview/">The International site</a>, where you&#8217;ll find replays for every game. You can also check out photos and videos from the event on <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/dota2ti/">Flickr</a> and at our <a href="http://www.dota2.com/international/media/">media page</a>.</p>
<p>We&#8217;d like to thank all of the teams, and everyone in the Dota community for helping to bring year&#8217;s tournament to life. Like you, we look forward to The International every year, and without you this amazing event wouldn&#8217;t be possible.</p>
