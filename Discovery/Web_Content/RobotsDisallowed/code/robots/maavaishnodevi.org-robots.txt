<h3 style="color:#FFFF66; text-align:center;">Important Information: must read before online booking</h3>

<p style="color:#FFFFFF; font-size:12px; font-family:Arial; text-align:justify">www.maavaishnodevi.org is the only official website of Shri Mata Vaishno Devi Shrine Board (SMVDSB). Pilgrims must approach this site only for seeking online services. Other websites (with resembling names), trying to offer various online services including helicopter tickets are not recognized by the Shrine Board. Pilgrims are very strongly advised not to approach such websites (with resembling names) for seeking any online service of SMVDSB.</p>
 
<p style="color:#FFFFFF; font-size:12px; font-family:Arial; text-align:justify">Pilgrims are forewarned that approaching such websites and booking online for helicopter tickets or other services of Shri Mata Vaishno Devi Shrine Board might lead to their being cheated or inconvenienced in some form. The responsibility in this regard shall rest entirely with the concerned pilgrim(s).</p>

<p style="color:#FFFFFF; font-size:12px; font-family:Arial; text-align:justify">The helicopter tickets are available only @Rs.1,039/- per person per side and there is no other rate/commission/processing fee etc. whatsoever per ticket. Pilgrims trying such methods to secure helicopter tickets etc. shall do so at their own risk and responsibility.</p>
