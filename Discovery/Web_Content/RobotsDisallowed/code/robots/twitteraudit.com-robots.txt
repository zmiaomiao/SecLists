      <h2>About TwitterAudit</h2>
      <p>Each audit takes a random sample of 5000 Twitter followers for a user and calculates a score for each follower. This score is based on <b>number of tweets</b>, <b>date of the last tweet</b>, and <b>ratio of followers to friends</b>. We use these scores to determine whether any given user is real or fake. Of course, this scoring method is not perfect but it is a good way to tell if someone with lots of followers is likely to have increased their follower count by inorganic, fraudulent, or dishonest means.</p>
