              <div class="large-10">
                  <h3>Mosquito Watch</h3>
                   Mosquitoes thrive during the warm summer months and are most prevalent around ponds, swamps and other standing bodies of water.
              </div>
            </div>

                </div>
                <div class="large-10">
                    <h3>Hornets</h3>
                     These social insects are most aggressive during the late summer and fall, when their food sources drastically decrease and they’re forced to forage.
                </div>
            </div>

                <div class="large-10">
                    <h3>Spiders</h3>
                     Depending on the species, spiders can prefer different climates. Some seek damp basements and crawlspaces, while others prefer dry, warm areas.
