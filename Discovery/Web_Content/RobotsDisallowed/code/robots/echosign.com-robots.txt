<h2 class="text-normal&#x20;text-4-block&#x20;text-padding-t2x">What are electronic and digital signatures&#x3f;</h2></div>
        
        
    
        <div>

<div class="text-padding-t5x&#x20;dc-text">
  <div class="text-5-block"><span class="text-light">Electronic signatures, also known as ‘e-signatures’, remove the need for ink signatures. They help organizations transact business faster and reduce risk through greater automation, control and visibility. For signers, they offer a simple and legally valid way to indicate consent or approval on a digital document or form. A digital signature is a specific implementation of an e-signature that required the signer to have a certificated-based digital ID. Adobe eSign services supports both electronic and digital signatures.</span><br />
</div>
