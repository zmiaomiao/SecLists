	<h2 class="post-title"><a href="http://www.naijatechguide.com/2015/09/amazon-fire-tv-gaming-edition-specs.html">Amazon Fire TV Gaming Edition Specs & Price</a></h2>

    
    

    <div class="post-body">
<div id="mobileonly" style="border-style:solid; border-width:1px; border-color: C0C0C0; height: 83px;">

<div itemprop="articleBody">
<div style="clear:both;"></div><p>This is a variant of the new Amazon Fire TV designed specifically with gamers in mind. The Fire TV Gaming Edition offers basically everything on the regular edition and more, including a redesigned game controller with voice support and two free games.</p><div class="shop"><h2>Where to Buy Fire TV Gaming Edition</h2><span itemprop="offers" itemscope itemtype="http://schema.org/Offer"><span style="padding-right: 30px;">Amazon.com</span><span itemprop="price" style="padding-right: 30px;">$139.99</span> <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00XNQECFM/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&camp=1789&creative=390957&creativeASIN=B00XNQECFM&linkCode=as2&tag=naijatechguide-20&linkId=462DY4IRLOALKHHC" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><b>View Offer</b></a><img src="http://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=naijatechguide-20&l=as2&o=1&a=B00XNQECFM" width="1" height="1" border="0" alt="" style="border:none !important; margin:0px !important;" /></span></div><br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00XNQECFM/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&camp=1789&creative=390957&creativeASIN=B00XNQECFM&linkCode=as2&tag=naijatechguide-20&linkId=462DY4IRLOALKHHC" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><img alt="Amazon Fire TV Gaming Edition" border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-QA-rHa9mHaQ/VgFdnq1nzWI/AAAAAAAAPvQ/vgPAIiGPbys/s1600/amazon-fire-tv-gaming-edition.jpg" /></a></div>Although the device is focused on gaming, it is an online media streaming device to the core with access to over 250,000 movies and TV episodes from the likes Amazon Video, Netflix, Hulu and many more services.<br />
<span class="fullpost"><br />
<h2>Awesome Gaming Performance</h2>Amazon Fire TV Gaming Edition is configured to provide breathtaking gaming performance, being equipped with a robust processor – very likely the same 64-bit MediaTek quad-core processor (clocking at 2GHz) that powers the new Fire TV.<br />
<br />
According to Amazon, this means users are in for 75% boost in processing power. The micro-console comes with a dedicated GPU to make for really superb gaming experience and better gaming capability than ‘any other streaming media box’ can offer.<br />
<br />
There are over 800 games available online for the Fire TV. According to Amazon this is the largest number of gaming titles for any streaming media player. Some titles that Amazon is promoting include Minecraft, Lego Star Wars, and Pac-Man 256.<br />
<br />
The Fire TV Gaming Edition is bundled with two free games (valued at $24.98). The two games are the award-winning Shovel Knight and critically-acclaimed Disney DuckTales: Remastered<br />
<h2>Upgraded Game Controller</h2>The new Fire TV comes with an improved game controller – bundled with the Gaming Edition but offered as an accessory for the regular <a href="http://www.naijatechguide.com/2015/09/amazon-fire-tv-specs-price-movies.html"><b>Fire TV</b></a> model. The new game controller parades a more ergonomic design and a feel similar to what you would expect of a regular game console controller.<br />
<br />
It features a Dolby technology-supported audio jack for private listening. The game controller has integrated microphone to allow you use voice controls for search and Amazon’s personal assistant Alexa. It also boasts improved battery life of up to 90 hours.<br />
<h2>Additional Information</h2>Buyers of Amazon Fire TV Gaming Edition will be able to get two games – Shovel Knight and Disney’s Ducktales – with their purchase. The retail giant says it has raised its game offerings by about 500 percent, with buyers able to get more than half of the available games at 99 cents or less each.<br />
<br />
Fire TV Gaming Edition is capable of handling 4K UHD content like the regular edition. In addition to the inbuilt storage of 8 GB, there is a microSD card slot and a 32GB card is already provided by Amazon.<br />
<br />
You will possibly be able to expand storage by as much as 128 GB, which will be a plus over the Apple TV. The box offers longer battery life and supports dual-band Wi-Fi with MIMO.<br />

	<h2 class="post-title"><a href="http://www.naijatechguide.com/2015/09/samsung-smartthings-hub-2015.html">Samsung SmartThings Hub (2015)</a></h2>

    
    

    <div class="post-body">
<div id="mobileonly" style="border-style:solid; border-width:1px; border-color: C0C0C0; height: 83px;">
</div>


<div itemprop="articleBody">
<div style="clear:both;"></div><p>Samsung SmartThings Hub (2015) is the second-generation Internet of Things (IoT) hub from SmartThings, a company owned by Samsung. Presented at IFA 2015, the new Samsung SmartThings Hub comes with an improved app, extra connectivity option and long-hour battery backup.</p><br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-gcUgYbtWRLY/Vf2Q-eSandI/AAAAAAAAPuU/ahP8uX71WA4/s1600/samsung-smartthings-hub-2015.jpg" /></div><span class="fullpost"><br />
<h2>About the Hub</h2>The Internet of Things, same as the Smart Home concept, refers to the idea of internet-connected devices in a home being able to communicate with one another for efficiency – for instance, a door telling the bulbs to turn off when you are off to work.<br />
<br />
However, IoT devices are made by different manufacturers, with this creating problem of incompatibility. Samsung SmartThings Hub is designed to ensure the compatibility of these devices from diverse makers to make a smart home feasible.<br />
<br />
So, the the SmartThings Hub helps all these IoT devices talk to each other.<br />
<h2>The Design</h2>The SmartThings Hub (2015) has a somewhat similar look as the last-generation hub. It still features a square-shaped design with a plastic body and nicely curved corners.<br />
<br />
The sensors look smaller and sleeker. SmartThings says the sensors are redesigned to allow users add a more contemporary feel to their living space.<br />
<h2>Superior App</h2>The IoT hub comes with an upgraded app which users will find more intuitive and stress-free to use. Users are now able to organise and control connected devices according to rooms where they are situated – almost 200 devices are compatible with the Samsung SmartThings Hub (2015).<br />
<br />
The app features a new integrated home monitoring solution called Smart Home Monitor, which alerts you and enable you take action when a situation requiring urgent attention in your home crops up. The Smart Home Monitor can send alerts with video clips of events that happen.<br />
<br />
The improved SmartThings app will be available for iOS, Android and Windows Phone platforms.<br />
<h2>Other Details</h2>Samsung SmartThings Hub (2015) now features an embedded Bluetooth 4.0 adapter along with the existing Wi-Fi, ZigBee and Z-Wave radios to make it possible to hook up more devices to the system.<br />
<br />
There are two USB ports on the hub. According to its maker, the Smart Home hub boasts battery backup of up to 10 hours, which could be quite useful when there is a power outage.<br />
