                <span class="trebuchetbold12pix style1">Pixie Trix Comix launches today <a href="http://www.stickydillybuns.com/">Sticky Dilly Buns</a>, a new comic starring Dillon of Ma3. The cast will also include Ma3's Amber & Chanelle, and new characters will gradually be introduced as well. The creative team on the comic will consist of me and my long-time Eerie Cuties collaborator, <a href="http://www.fragilestory.com/">Shouri</a>. We'll be updating it every Monday and Friday. We hope you'll check it out, and include it in your webcomic reads. Before closing, Chloe of Go Get A Roomie is having a <a href="http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/1328500402/go-get-a-roomie-book-one">Kickstarter</a>, go help her out!--Gisele :)</span>

<span class="trebuchetbold12pix style1">

                  <br/><strong>NOVEMBER 20, 2012 <br/></strong>

                </span>

                <span class="trebuchetbold12pix style1">We are now taking pre-orders for <a href="http://pixietrixcomixstore.bigcartel.com/">our latest volume</a> of Eerie Cuties (w/Magick Chicks). We expect to ship the book mid-December. Like our past volumes, we include a bonus story and collector postcards. We're also running a little holiday sale on past volumes, so if you need to catch up, now's a good time. All the details on what's inside the book is at our store. You can also preview a bonus page from the bonus story. Pre-orders are important for us to determine how big to make our print run, so please, if you're interested in getting the book, considering pre-ordering it. It really does help us out. Thanks everyone!--Gisele :)</span>

                </span>

                <span class="trebuchetbold12pix style1">The Eerie Cuties/Magick Chicks universe gets a new spin-off today: <a href="http://www.dangerouslychloe.com">Dangerously Chloe!</a> Chloe will be a familiar face to Eerie Cuties readers, but knowing Chloe's history isn't mandatory to follow this series. The update schedule will be Mondays and Thursdays. It'll be written by Dave (from EC/MC) and drawn by Cassandra Wedeking, the wonderful artist on <a href="http://www.magickchicks.com">Magick Chicks</a>. I think it's fair to say it's in good hands. We're really happy with this new project, and we hope you'll bookmark it and make it part of your webcomic reading. To all our EC/MC readers, thanks for all the support you've given us over the years!</a>--Gisele :)</span></div>
