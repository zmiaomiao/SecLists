  <section id="our-story" class="centeredContent">
    <h1>Our Story</h1>
    <div class="dynamicContent">
<div class="item">
	<img alt="Our Beginning" src="/getattachment/288952ed-ffcd-4768-94a7-32dd5bd72cd5/Jimmy-Dean-Introduces-A-New-Product" width="380" height="250"/>
	<section>
		<h3>1996</h3>
		<h2>Our Beginning</h2>
		<p>Optimum Media Direction (OMD) launches in France with the unification of BBDO, DDB and TBWA media operations.  OMD Mexico quickly follows and the network is born.</p>
	</section>
</div><div class="item">
	<img alt="The Year of Asia Pacific" src="/getattachment/f41587ed-2b79-4870-bcc5-6f2536a64e57/Someone-special-is-born" width="380" height="250"/>
	<section>
		<h3>1998</h3>
		<h2>The Year of Asia Pacific</h2>
		<p>OMD formally establishes APAC operations opening offices in Hong Kong, China and Taiwan.  OMD Hungary opens with Pepsi and Vodafone as foundation clients.</p>
	</section>
</div><div class="item">
	<img alt="Our first Gold Lion in Cannes" src="/getattachment/a6a9c726-1836-4b13-bef9-f8e87f130191/OMD-CREATES-NEW-WEB-SITE" width="380" height="250"/>
	<section>
		<h3>1999</h3>
		<h2>Our first Gold Lion in Cannes</h2>
		<p>OMD Hungary wins the agency’s first gold Lion at Cannes for Vodafone’s launch campaign.  New offices open in Singapore, Thailand, Malaysia, Czech Republic, Greece and Poland.</p>
	</section>
</div><div class="item">
	<img alt="OMD EMEA Launch" src="/getattachment/ebfc13d6-0181-469b-803a-4909a4c66f9c/The-EMEA-launch" width="380" height="250"/>
	<section>
		<h3>2001</h3>
		<h2>OMD EMEA Launch</h2>
		<p>All Omnicom media buying operations across EMEA, united under the OMD brand.  OMD opens offices in Argentina expanding LATAM regional capability.</p>
	</section>
</div><div class="item">
	<img alt="OMD Global Agency of the Year" src="/getattachment/f90647a5-3055-473a-b9fc-ec1ce47f0d78/Recma-names-OMD-as-the-largest-media-agency-in-the" width="380" height="250"/>
	<section>
		<h3>2003</h3>
		<h2>OMD Global Agency of the Year</h2>
		<p>OMD named "Global Agency of the Year" by Advertising Age.  RECMA names OMD as the world’s largest media network with billings of $19.3 billion.  OMD formally establishes LATAM operations in Miami.  </p>
	</section>
</div><div class="item">
	<img alt="OMD Global Agency of the Year" src="/getattachment/20a43e5b-d26c-41bf-9f13-45949f57d1e6/OMD-named-Global-Agency-of-the-Year-on-both-sides" width="380" height="250"/>
	<section>
		<h3>2005</h3>
		<h2>OMD Global Agency of the Year</h2>
		<p>OMD Colombia wins three Gold Lions in Cannes, the most won by an individual media agency.  OMD named “Global Agency of the Year” by Advertising Age.</p>
	</section>
</div><div class="item">
	<img alt="OMD Global Agency of the Year" src="/getattachment/0cd8236c-ebbb-4812-9d46-7096f33515de/OMD-named-regional-Media-Agency-of-the-Year-in-bot" width="380" height="250"/>
	<section>
		<h3>2006</h3>
		<h2>OMD Global Agency of the Year</h2>
		<p>OMD named Global Media Agency of the Year by Adweek and US Agency of the Year by Advertising Age.  OMD maintains its position as the world’s largest media network with billings in excess of $26.3 billion.  </p>
	</section>
</div><div class="item">
	<img alt="OMD India Opens" src="/getattachment/5f657958-8491-4031-9536-86c1820330e2/OMD-India-Opens" width="380" height="250"/>
	<section>
		<h3>2007</h3>
		<h2>OMD India Opens</h2>
		<p>For the third year in a row, OMD retains its Gunn Report for Media title and is named the most competitive agency in the world by RECMA.  OMD India opens with offices in Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore and Chennai.</p>
	</section>
</div><div class="item">
	<img alt="OMD Global Agency of the Year" src="/getattachment/353be569-c216-4b92-8a50-c912d1c904de/The-year-of-Vodafone" width="380" height="250"/>
	<section>
		<h3>2009</h3>
		<h2>OMD Global Agency of the Year</h2>
		<p>OMD awarded US Media Agency of the Year by Advertising Age and Global Media Agency of the Year by Adweek.</p>
	</section>
</div><div class="item">
	<img alt="OMD Global Agency of the Year" src="/getattachment/fde2ddcb-4c94-4906-a562-4ebbaec90249/OMD-Global-Agency-of-the-Year" width="380" height="250"/>
	<section>
		<h3>2011</h3>
		<h2>OMD Global Agency of the Year</h2>
		<p>OMD wins more Cannes Lions and Effies than any other media agency globally.  OMD named Global Agency of the Year by Adweek and US Agency of the Year by Advertising Age.</p>
	</section>
</div><div class="item">
	<img alt="A Fresh Look" src="/getattachment/a3cce849-335c-4d7a-b763-927176909244/OMD-Fast-Forward" width="380" height="250"/>
	<section>
		<h3>2012</h3>
		<h2>A Fresh Look</h2>
		<p>OMD launches with a fresh look around the world celebrating over 15 years of extraordinary accomplishments.  Find out how you can join and be a part of our story in 2012!</p>
	</section>
