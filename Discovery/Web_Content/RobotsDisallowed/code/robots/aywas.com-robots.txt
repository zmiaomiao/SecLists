The foxes have gotten a foxy upgrade! When submitting to the <a href="http://www.aywas.com/fauxbox/">Faux Box</a> feature, you are now permitted to select one of your designs as an "auto-claim" in exchange  for your submission.

This means that players can freely commission Faux sets and have one designed for themselves and claim that design when submitting it to the box. It also means that artists no longer have to stalk the upload times to make sure they get the one they want from their own set. Just submit, select your design to claim, and send it off!

Note that if you select a design to claim, you will not receive a Fox in the Box. The auto-claim is considered the reward for the submission.

In addition, submissions can now be rejected with a rejection reason. This means that if your Faux submission is rejected you'll know what to fix.
