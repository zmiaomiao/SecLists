<div class="docbook-para">Yandex.Money is an online payment service. We work around the clock alongside tens of thousands of Russian and international stores so that users can pay anytime and anywhere for mobile services, internet, Skype, games, tickets, home utilities, and lots of other goods and services.</div>
 
<div class="docbook-para">We are constantly adding new ways for users to <a href="/prepaid/">add money to Yandex.Money accounts</a>. Users can deposit money at bank branches, mobile retailers, payment kiosks, and other points. Most methods are commission free. Even if a user's Yandex.Money account balance is empty, the user can pay from a <a href="/card/card-payment/about.xml">linked card</a>. We save the card's details in our system, so there's no need to enter them again at checkout. </div>
 
<div class="docbook-para">Yandex.Money helps its users raise funds for projects and charities, by providing them with user-friendly and eye-catching <a href="/embed/">tools</a>, like our Facebook and VKontakte apps. After raising funds, users can <a href="/doc.xml?id=157131">withdraw</a> money from their accounts or send it to other users.</div>
 
<div class="docbook-para">We also offer a <a href="/ymc/promo.xml"> free Yandex.Money MasterCard</a>, that allows users to pay with money from their Yandex.Money accounts anywhere where MasterCard is accepted.</div>

<div class="docbook-para"><a href="http://kassa.yandex.ru/">Yandex Payment Solution</a> is a universal tool for accepting payments online. Merchants can implement our Payment Solution to start accepting payments to a bank account by credit card and other popular methods.</div>
 
<div class="docbook-para">To find out more about these and other options, visit our <a href="/services/">Services</a> page.</div>
