<h2>About OMG!</h2>
<div class="hr"><span><strong></strong></span></div>
<p class="subtitle last-heading">
OrderMyGear is the <strong class="highlight">industry leader</strong><br> for <strong class="highlight">online team stores</strong> and group ordering
</p>
<div class="row">
<div class="col-md-6">
<h5>For Dealers</h5>
<p>
Sell more gear and simplify your team sales by partnering with OrderMyGear. On average, our dealers see their team sales jump more than 30% after switching to OrderMyGear online stores. With more than $100 million in sales and 70,000 stores behind us, we have the experience and platform to help take your business to the next level.Our company was born out of the frustration of paper trails, bounced checks, and countless hours spent calculating order totals. The first software company of its kind, OrderMyGear is the benchmark for quality, customer service, and innovation as we help grow team business for our dealers.Schedule a free demo and find out how we can help you sell more gear with no monthly fees and no per-store cost to you.
<br><a href="#freedemo">Request a Free Demo</a>
</p>
</div>
<div class="col-md-6">
<h5>For Teams</h5>
<p>
Whether you're the coach, a team mom, booster club leader, or a player tasked with finding the right gear for your team, OrderMyGear makes it easy. Our dedicated product specialists will help you select the products you need, customize an online store for your team, notify your players so they can choose their own sizes and options and pay individually, then deliver your order as a group either using your favorite existing local dealer or one from our marketplace of preferred dealer partners. Teams love our built-in fundraising tools, automatic payment processing, and how easy we make the whole process. In fact, on average our teams and coaches spend 78% less time managing gear using OrderMyGear.
<br>

</p>
