<h2>Flexible hotspot management options</h2>
<p>Whether your business has one location or thousands, AT&amp;T Wi-Fi services
can provide the connectivity your guests and customers expect. As the premier
business Wi-Fi service provider for retail, hospitality, dining, sports
entertainment, and airports, AT&amp;T offers a wide range of Wi-Fi services and
hotspot management offerings to meet your requirements and budget.</p>
<h2>Choose the AT&amp;T Wi-Fi solution that fits your business needs</h2>
<p>Choose the Wi-Fi solution that fits your business needs.</p>
<h3>
<a href="/enterprise/Service/network-services/wifi-solutions/ready-zone/">AT&amp;T
Ready Zone</a>
</h3>
<p>
<a href="/enterprise/Service/network-services/wifi-solutions/ready-zone/">Ready
Zone</a> is AT&amp;T&#8217;s low cost, self-installed and managed public Wi-Fi
solution designed for small venues. AT&amp;T Ready Zone:</p>
<ul>
<li>Includes a Wi-Fi access point at the site location.</li>
<li>Includes a cloud gateway, offering public Wi-Fi services only.</li>
<li>Installation is limited to a single access point.</li>
<li>If you choose, AT&amp;T can install the equipment for you for a fee.</li>
</ul>
<h3>AT&amp;T Connect Link</h3>
<p>Connect Link offers three service levels to provide the right level of
monitoring, management and maintenance for your enterprise business. These
options are:</p>
<h4>Connect Link Base</h4>
<p>If your business has Wi-Fi but no longer wishes to manage and maintain it,
Connect Link Base is a good option. AT&amp;T installs a gateway on your
premises and integrates with the existing WLAN to offer public Wi-Fi
services.</p>
<p>AT&amp;T manages the gateway, and you are responsible for managing and
configuring the WLAN to enable guest access.</p>
<ul>
<li>The gateway-only service option can support limited private Wi-Fi services
(e.g., WAN Failover capability for additional VLANs).</li>
<li>An optional switch is available. Switches identify the intended destination
of the information that they receive, so they send that information to only the
computers that are supposed to receive it.</li>
<li>24/7 equipment monitoring is provided, subject to periodic downtime for
routine maintenance.</li>
</ul>
<h4>Connect Link Select</h4>
<p>Great for hotels and hospitality venues, Connect Link Select allows you to
pick and choose the level of support your business needs. AT&amp;T installs the
Wi-Fi service gateway on your premises and provides monitor-only capabilities
for your existing WLAN. Depending on the level of service you request, AT&amp;T
can alert you or perform billable maintenance if an issue arises on your WLAN.
With this service:</p>
<ul>
<li>Gateway and WLAN custom configuration options are available</li>
<li>24/7 equipment monitoring is provided, subject to periodic downtime of
monitoring systems for routine maintenance.</li>
</ul>
<p>
<em>Note: Connect Link Select is currently only available in hospitality on
SIMS.</em>
</p>
<h3>Connect Link Total</h3>
<p>If your business requires a fully managed Wi-Fi option, <a href="/binary/content/productbrochures/Wi-Fi-Hospitality-att-connectlink-total.pdf" target="_blank">Connect Link Total</a> [PDF, 2MB] is the choice for you.
AT&amp;T installs the gateway and WLAN equipment on your premises and provides
full monitoring and maintenance services.</p>
<p>A managed WLAN and Gateway is provided on premises with the option to
provide both public and back office functionality. Public functionality allows
access to anyone you WANT on your network while visiting your premises. Back
office functionality could be applications that manage inventory, security,
monitoring the network, etc. With this service:</p>
<ul>
<li>Repair and maintenance for AT&amp;T equipment is included for the contract
term.</li>
<li>Customization is offered in both the gateway and WLAN configurations.</li>
<li>24/7 equipment monitoring is provided, subject to periodic downtime of
monitoring systems for routine maintenance.</li>
</ul>
<h2>Why AT&amp;T for your business Wi-Fi service?</h2>
<p>As a Wi-Fi Hotspot provider, AT&amp;T can:</p>
<ul>
<li>Provide multiple Wi-Fi hotspot management options that fit your unique
business needs.</li>
<li>Customize your Wi-Fi design to provide a unique experience for your
customers. This could include adding visuals and logos, co-branded splash
pages, loyalty program and mobile application integration, and more to enhance
your customers' Wi-Fi experience.</li>
<li>Subject to user privacy controls we can provide user analytics capabilities
that can enable you to learn more about your customers.</li>
<li>Include dedicated client service and customer care call centers to meet
your Wi-Fi service and hotspot management needs.</li>
<li>Provide IPv6 transition assistance. <a href="/enterprise/online_campaign/ipv6/">Learn how</a>.</li>
</ul>
<p>Learn more about how AT&amp;T can help you with Wi-Fi hotspot solution
planning and installation for your business, and about other solutions for <a href="/enterprise/Family/mobility-services/mobile-devices/">Mobile Devices</a>,
<a href="/enterprise/Family/network-services/internet-connectivity/">Internet
Connectivity</a>, VPN Connections, <a href="/enterprise/Service/network-services/ip-vpn/remote-access/">Remote
Access</a>, and <a href="/enterprise/Portfolio/network-security/">Network
